# 🗺️ Super Cool MiniMap Plugin! 🚀

Hi! This is the **MiniMap** plugin made by **Fusion Developer**! 

Have you ever played a game and gotten lost? Me too! That's why I made this little map that goes in the corner of your screen. It's like having a pet bird that flies high up in the sky and looks down at your player to tell you where you are! 🦅

## 🌟 What does it do?
* **It watches you:** It puts a tiny screen inside your game screen! 📺
* **It follows you:** No matter where your player runs, the camera follows them from above. 🏃‍♂️
* **It spins (if you want!):** You can tell the map to spin around when your player turns, or tell it to stay perfectly still. 🔄
* **It looks awesome:** It has a super cool logo called the **Fusion Core**! ⚛️

## 🛠️ How to put it in your Godot Game!
1. Put the whole `minimap` folder inside your `addons` folder.
2. Go up to the top of Godot and click **Project** -> **Project Settings** -> **Plugins**.
3. Find **MiniMap** in the list and click the little checkmark box to turn it on! ✅
4. Now, go to your game scene, press `Ctrl + A` and type **MiniMap** to add it.
5. Look on the right side of the screen (in the Inspector) and tell it who your player is by clicking **Follow Object**.
6. Press Play and enjoy your new map! 🎮

Have so much fun making your games! 🎈
